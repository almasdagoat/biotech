The da Vinci Surgical System is a renowned robotic surgical system crafted by Intuitive Surgical that serves a wide range of purposes in the medical field like prostatectomies or cardiac valve repair as well as gynecologic surgeries. Patients can potentially benefit from this system with quicker recovery times after surgeries while experiencing lower blood loss aside from having reduced chances of developing complications along their recovery journey.

   However even with these notable advantages comes some accompanying risks that must be considered attentively. Incidents linked to electrical arcing alongside improper marketing tactics utilized exclusively on specific procedures have arisen which underscores the paramount importance of adhering steadfastly to all safety protocols prescribed during operations.    Moreover there are concerns that must not go unnoticed regarding possible payments made by Intuitive Surgical towards doctors' research efforts that could inadvertently bias their results.

While the da Vinci Surgical System as it stands is the singular FDA approved robotic surgical robot designed specifically for a multitude of procedures alternative robotic systems do exist that cater to unique surgical objectives. Nonetheless there remains a discrepancy in the consistent reporting of negative events linked to robotic surgery which should merit consideration when assessing risks.    Instances of adverse effects inclusive of fatalities have been recorded and are associated with the da Vinci robot specifically. Risks linked to robotic surgery including those involving the da Vinci system can potentially involve device malfunctions in addition to cases where operations need to be switched from closed to open or cause consequences as accidental injuries inflicted on surrounding organs.
